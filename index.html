Privacy Policy

SlickShot: App Screenshot Maker
Effective Date: May 25, 2026

Welcome to SlickShot: App Screenshot Maker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web services.

By using SlickShot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data

  • Account Information: If you create an account, sign in via email, or upgrade to a premium subscription, we collect your email address and authentication tokens to manage your cloud projects and sync data.
  • Payment Information: We use third-party payment processors like Stripe and Google Play Billing for subscription management and one-time purchases (e.g., Pro Unlock). We do not collect or store your credit card or financial details on our servers. All transaction details are handled securely by these payment gateways.

B. Device and Usage Data

  • Local Storage & Project Data: Your mockups, custom gradients, layout templates, and preferences are stored locally on your device via localStorage or saved securely to your cloud workspace if you are a premium subscriber.
  • Native Device Features:
    • Media/Gallery Access: The app requests access to your device's photo gallery/storage solely to allow you to upload screenshots for mockup generation and to save the final exported images directly to your device.
    • Haptic Feedback & Orientation: We utilize device hardware features for vibration feedback and screen orientation management to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the application features.
  • To manage your user account, cloud projects, and team workspaces.
  • To process your payments and handle subscription upgrades.
  • To save generated mockups directly to your device gallery upon your explicit request.
  • To monitor and improve the overall user experience and application stability.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Local Data: Free tier users' configuration data and export limits remain strictly on their local device storage.

Cloud Data: Premium and account-holder project data are securely hosted on cloud servers (e.g., Render/Databases). We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide you with active cloud project synchronization features.

4. Third-Party Services

SlickShot integrates third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services / Play Billing: For app deployment and securing in-app purchases.
  • Stripe: For secure web payment processing and customer subscription portal management.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Security of Your Data

The security of your data is important to us. We implement industry-standard security measures (such as JWT tokens for authentication and secure HTTPS protocols) to protect your personal information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

SlickShot does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.
